# 👋 About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at [Ghent University](https://www.ugent.be/) and a combined M.Eng.-Ph.D. student in Computer Science at [Northeastern University](https://www.neu.edu.cn/), China.

My research interests lie broadly in computer vision and multimodal visual understanding, with a focus on vision-language models, camouflaged object understanding, remote sensing image interpretation, defect detection, and annotation-efficient learning.

# 🔍 Research Topics

- **Multimodal Visual Understanding**: Vision-language models, multimodal perception, and instruction-guided visual understanding.
- **Object Detection and Segmentation**: Camouflaged object understanding, salient object segmentation, defect detection, and pixel-level visual reasoning.
- **Remote Sensing and Industrial Vision**: Remote sensing image interpretation, complex-scene perception, and visual inspection.
- **Efficient and Annotation-Efficient Learning**: Weakly supervised learning, self-supervised learning, and low-cost visual model training.
